About the circuit

Baku joins the likes of Monaco, Melbourne and Singapore as a beloved street circuit. It’s first grand prix was held in 20016 as the European Grand Prix with it being changed to the Azerbaijan Grand Prix from 2017 onwards.

Its long main straight is a perfect opportunity for the current cars to display the prowess of DRS and get some much needed overtakes. The rest of the circuit loops around the citiy’s old town with a tight section between the new and old city walls.
