About the circuit

The Circuit of The Americas is another one of Hermann Tilke’s circuits. It took a number of years to be built due to some setbacks but finally had its opening in October of 2012

Its inaugural race was in 2012. It had been 4 seasons since Formula 1 last raced in the USA with the previous race being held in the 2007 season at Indianapolis.

Its famous S style section between turns 3 and 6 are resonant of Silverstone’s Maggotts and Becketts or Suzuka’s S Curves. The circuit is a fan favourite for providing some of the best overtaking of the season.
