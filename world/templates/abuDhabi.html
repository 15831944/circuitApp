About the circuit

In 2006, planning went underway to develop a circuit on Yas Island, to the east of Abu Dhabi. In 2007 construction began on the 5.5km racetrack with it being completed in October of 2009.

The inaugural race was held in 2009 with Sebastian Vettel taking first place, followed by teammate Mark Webber with the previous years’ champion; Jensen Button close behind.

The circuit however, is more than just a measly racetrack. Its original plan was to turn the whole of Yas Island into a tourist attraction. After 11 years and over $40 billion later, the track is now home to seven hotels, a mall, water park, a golf course, a beach, concert arena and Ferrari’s very own indoor theme park.
